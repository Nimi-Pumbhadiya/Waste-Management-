Service Info

Service Information

Heavy Machinery

Industrial vacuum trucks (5000-gallon capacity), high-pressure washing systems (40,000PSI), leak-proof containment vehicles, and crane-mounted cleaning equipment.

All equipment undergoes regular maintenance and calibration to ensure optimal performance and compliance with industry standards.

Safety & Testing Equipment

Level A-D protective suits & respirators, real-time air quality monitors, emergency decontamination stations, and portable chemical analysis instruments.

Our safety protocols exceed OSHA requirements with continuous monitoring and immediate response capabilities for hazardous situations.

Efficient Waste Solutions

Manual Sorting

Experienced staff manually sort materials by type, ensuring proper separation and maximizing recycling potential with careful attention to detail.

Documentation System

Comprehensive paper-based tracking system with detailed logs and manifests for complete regulatory compliance and audit trails.

Direct Communication

Personal phone and email support with dedicated account managers providing regular updates and scheduling coordination.

Hazardous Materials

Chemical solvents, acids, toxic byproducts, contaminated equipment, and radioactive materials. We maintain RCRA permits and follow strict chain-of-custody documentation for all hazardous waste streams.

Examples:
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Lead-based paint waste
  • Mercury-containing devices

Industrial Waste

Oil and petroleum products, metal shavings, manufacturing residues, and process wastewater. Our recycling partnerships ensure maximum material recovery and cost-effective disposal solutions.

Examples:
  • Scrap metals and alloys
  • Used machinery oils
  • Production line waste

Organic & Chemical Waste

Food processing waste, biodegradable materials, laboratory chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and cleaning agents. We provide composting solutions and chemical neutralization services.

Examples:
  • Expired pharmaceuticals
  • Laboratory reagents
  • Organic solvents

Construction Waste

Demolition debris, concrete, steel, wood, and construction materials requiring specialized handling and recycling processes.

Examples:
  • Concrete and masonry debris
  • Steel beams and rebar
  • Wood framing materials

Pre-Operation Safety

Comprehensive site hazard assessment including atmospheric testing, PPE verification and fitting with Level A-D protection, emergency response plan development with evacuation routes, and equipment safety checks with calibration procedures.

Site Assessment
  • Atmospheric testing
  • Safety Hazard Detection
  • Risk evaluation
PPE Verification
  • Equipment inspection
  • Proper fitting
  • Functionality testing
Emergency Planning
  • Evacuation routes
  • Communication protocols
  • Response procedures

Operational Standards

Continuous air quality monitoring with real-time alarms, proper containment and isolation of hazardous areas, strict OSHA 29 CFR 1910.120 HAZWOPER compliance, buddy system implementation, and medical surveillance for all personnel.
